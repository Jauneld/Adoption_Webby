Know Who You Are Dealing With


Who Are We?


We’re more than an adoption agency—we’re storytellers of family, architects of belonging, and champions of love.

Our mission is simple yet profound: to build bridges between hearts.

We bring together children seeking a forever home and families yearning to grow. We love our jobs.

In our care, hope finds its home, dreams become reality, and love knows no bounds. That’s who we are.


Confidence in Every Step


At Loved Pets Animal Rescue, we believe every animal deserves a second chance at a life filled with love.

We start by providing essential veterinary care for each animal, ensuring they are healthy and ready for their forever home.

We’re the helping hand that guides you through this life-changing journey, offering support, and compassion at every step.

Then, we dedicate ourselves to matching each rescued pet with a family who will cherish and protect them.

This is the care our staff takes.
